Traditional Preparation

Traditional Desalted Selections

Our desalted cod and preserved seafood are prepared in-house the traditional way, restoring natural texture and flavor. The foundation of Greek "bakaliaros" dishes, done the way it has always been done.

Desalting takes care: the right number of water changes, steady temperature, and proper timing. Our fishmongers have been doing it for over 20 years. We prepare cod for Greek Easter and winter meals for families across the Northern Sporades.

Holiday Preparation

Lenten Selections

Fasting-approved seafood for Clean Monday, Holy Week, and the entire Lenten period. We scale up our stock for these major Greek Orthodox observances.

During Clean Monday (Καθαρά Δευτέρα) and Holy Week, we supply a wide range of shellfish, invertebrates, cephalopods, and preserved specialties from the Aegean Sea. From family gatherings to church community events across the island, you can get what you need without compromising on quality during the fasting period.

Peak Seasons We Serve

Clean Monday

February / March

The start of Lent. We stock up on shellfish, invertebrates, and preserved seafood for the traditional outdoor kite-flying feasts.

Holy Week

April

The busiest period on the Greek Orthodox calendar. We prepare desalted cod, shellfish, and fasting selections at peak volume for families and churches.

Summer Peak

June – September

Tourist season brings peak demand from villas, yachts, and restaurants across Skiathos. Our wholesale operation runs at full capacity to serve the island with the best Aegean seafood.
